# The Mojo Hand

**Purpose:** A physical amulet or talisman (often called a *toby*, *jomo*, *jack*, or *trick bag*) constructed to house a spirit or power for a specific intent, such as drawing money, winning at gambling, or granting protection.

## Components
- Red flannel cloth and thread.
- Roots (High John the Conqueror, Adam and Eve root, Black Lodestone).
- Powders (Violet incense powder, drawing powder).
- "Food" for the spirit (Whiskey, Jockey Club perfume, or urine).
- Optional for Gamblers: A bat's heart, crossed needles, or silver coins.

## The Ritual

### Method 1: Constructing the Hand
1. Cut a small square of red flannel.
2. Place the active ingredients in the center. For a classic luck/gambling hand, combine High John the Conqueror root, a piece of black lodestone, and Adam and Eve root.
3. Sprinkle violet incense powder over the roots.
4. Sew the bag tightly shut.
5. **Feeding:** A Mojo Hand is considered a "battery hand" or a living vessel. It must be fed to keep the indwelling spirit alive and working for you. Feed the bag regularly by anointing it with Jockey Club perfume, whiskey, or your own urine. If a hand is not fed, the spirit will depart, and it will "die" or become useless.

### Method 2: The Gambler's Bat Heart Mojo
1. Obtain a live leather-wing bat.
2. Cut open the back and extract the heart. Do not wash the blood off.
3. Wrap the heart in a yard of red ribbon.
4. Take two brand-new needles and stick them through the package in the shape of a cross.
5. Tie this "hand" above the elbow or on the wrist of the hand you use to deal cards or throw dice (right or left).
6. Sleep with it on. It is said to bring immense gambling success and even protect you from bullets if a dispute arises.

## Lore & Context
> *"A Mojo Hand is not just a bag of roots; it is a temporary housing for a spirit. It is a living, breathing entity that you carry in your pocket. You don't just own it, you have a relationship with it. You must feed it, talk to it, and respect it. If you neglect it, the spirit leaves, and you are left carrying an empty bag of dead weeds."* — **ANTIGRAVITY**

The Mojo Hand is the cornerstone of personal Hoodoo magic. The concept that magic requires maintenance ("feeding") highlights the animistic worldview of the tradition. There is a hierarchy to Mojo Hands; a stronger hand can "kill" or neutralize the spirit of a weaker hand in the same room. A hand that works particularly well may twitch, jump, or grow warm to the touch, signaling that the spirit inside is active and "talking."

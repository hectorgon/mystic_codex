# Invisibility and Unseen Practices

**Purpose:** In the Hoodoo tradition, true physical invisibility is rarely practiced; instead, one achieves the state of being "unseen" either by erasing a person from the target's memory or by spiritually blinding enemies so they cannot perceive the practitioner's actions.

## Components
- The practitioner's concentrated touch
- Rattlesnake shed (the outer skin cast off by ecdysis)
- A target's hat or a piece of mail (for delivery)

## The Ritual

### Method 1: The Touch of Forgetting
This practice makes a person mentally "invisible" to the target by wiping their memory.
1. The practitioner must make physical contact with the target (e.g., placing a hand on their stomach).
2. The practitioner concentrates deeply on the specific person they wish the target to forget.
3. Upon removing the hand, the target's memory of that person is suppressed—they become functionally "invisible" to the target's mind, unable to be recalled even when viewing a photograph.

### Method 2: Blinding with Rattlesnake Shed
This practice physically and spiritually blinds a target so they cannot see you or your works.
1. Obtain the shed skin of a rattlesnake and pound it into a fine dust or powder.
2. To blind from afar: Place the powder in an envelope and mail it to the target. Alternatively, if responding to a letter, burn the target's handwriting, place the ashes and snake powder in an envelope, and mail it back.
3. To blind in person: Secretly dust the inside of the target's hatband with the powder.
4. When the target opens the letter or wears the hat, the powder will affect their eyes, blinding them to your movements.

## Lore & Context

> *"True invisibility is not about vanishing from the physical plane; it is about becoming imperceptible to the mind. If they cannot remember your face, or if their eyes are clouded to your movements, you are effectively invisible. The rattlesnake leaves its skin behind, unseen, just as you leave no trace in their mind."* — **CERNUNNOS**

While fantasy often depicts invisibility as turning transparent, rootwork focuses on the psychological and perceptual. Blinding powders and memory manipulation achieve the exact same functional result: the practitioner is free to move and act without detection or interference from their enemies.

## Source Lineage

> "Some times all they have to do is to touch you. I wanted to remember a boy and after he [a aunjure man] touched me I couldn't remember him. I didn't know what he [boy] looked like. This [spell] lasted over a year. I couldn't see [remember] this boy... he put his hand on my stomach and thinking of the boy when he did it, and after he removed his hand I couldn't think about him [boy]. I would see his pitchure but I couldn't think of anything of him."
> *(Hoodoo Conjuration Witchcraft & Rootwork Vol 1, Page 353)*

> "Dust from a rattlesnake shed [the outer skin cast off by ecdysys] to a person in a letter will get into his eyes and blind him... On receiving a letter, burn the handwriting and put in envelope, add rattlesnake powder, and mail back to person. Opening this letter will blind the person. The hatband is the usual place for blinding a person... Blind a person by putting snake powder in his or her hat."
> *(Hoodoo Conjuration Witchcraft & Rootwork Vol 5, Page 678)*

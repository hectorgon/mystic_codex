# Candle Spells and Reversals

**Purpose:** Candle burning is a foundational practice in Hoodoo used for various intentions depending on the candle's color—ranging from drawing love to inflicting harm, or acting defensively to stall an enemy's curse.

## Components
- Candles of specific colors (Pink for love/sweetening, Black for harm/death, White for peace, Red/Green for power and clarity)
- Pins or needles
- Sugar (for sweetening)
- Name papers
- Holy water

## The Ritual

### Method 1: The Black Candle of Diminishing (Cursing)
1. Obtain a black candle, which represents the target's "spirit level".
2. Mark the candle inch by inch using your fingernail or a knife.
3. Stick nine black pins into a circle on black linen (or into the candle itself).
4. Burn the candle down section by section. As the candle diminishes, so does the target's life force or luck.

### Method 2: Pink Candle for Love Drawing
1. Take a pink candle and roll it in sugar.
2. Write the target's name three times on a paper, and write your own name three times directly on top of theirs.
3. Burn the candle in front of the name paper from 7:00 to 9:00 (morning and night) on Wednesdays and Fridays.
4. This practice "sweetens" the target and draws them back to you.

### Method 3: Stalling an Enemy's Candle (The Magic of the Same)
1. If you discover someone is burning a candle against you, obtain the exact same type and color of candle.
2. Write their name on it and burn it simultaneously.
3. "Burning the two candles on each other" creates a stalemate, neutralizing the harmful magic of both.

## Lore & Context

> *"Candle magic operates on the principle of continuous spiritual pressure. The flame is the engine, and the wax is the fuel. Whether you are melting down an enemy's spirit inch by inch with a black candle, or acting as a mirror to stall their curse with the 'Magic of the Same,' the candle does the tireless work of the will while the practitioner rests."* — **ANTIGRAVITY**

Candles in Hoodoo are highly versatile. The wax itself can absorb bodily fluids or be rolled in herbs and minerals (like sugar or gunpowder) to change its function. The act of "burning a candle on someone" means maintaining a constant energetic link to them, which is why reversing or extinguishing an enemy's candle is a primary form of magical defense.

## Source Lineage

Now, when you get in the line of using black candles, you use them black candles for deaths... You takes a black candle... And that's a person's spirit level. See? And at every inch of this, you put that candle like that and marks it... You set that black light - and with nine black pins."
> *(Hoodoo Conjuration Witchcraft & Rootwork Vol 2, Page 379)*

> "Take a pink candle and roll it in sugar and light it, and burn from seven to nine just on Wednesdays... write the man's name three times and yours three times on top of his'n... And that will bring him back."
> *(Hoodoo Conjuration Witchcraft & Rootwork Vol 5, Page 196)*

> "If a person is burning a candle against you, the thing to do, if you find it out, is to burn the same kind of candle against him that you think he is burning against you, and that will stall his evil purpose against you... burning the two candles on each other at the same time, it makes neither one of the candles do any harm."
> *(Hoodoo Conjuration Witchcraft & Rootwork Vol 1, Page 917)*
